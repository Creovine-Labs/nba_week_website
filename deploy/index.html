About NBA Week 2026

Africa's Premier Blockchain and AI Innovation Summit

Nigeria Blockchain and AI Week (NBA Week) is Nigeria's premier annual gathering spotlighting the convergence of blockchain technology, artificial intelligence, and crypto innovation. This event convenes industry leaders, policymakers, innovators, investors, and tech enthusiasts from across Nigeria, West Africa, and the global ecosystem.

Now in its sixth edition, NBA Week is proudly organized by CoinNewsExtra, Africa's number one Web3 TV, dedicated to blockchain, AI, and crypto, in collaboration with the Fintech Association of Nigeria (FAN).

NBA Week serves as a dynamic platform for thought leadership, strategic collaboration, policy dialogue, startup showcases, and product launches, accelerating Africa's position in the global emerging technology landscape.
